## Design & UX
- **Color scheme**: Professional blue tones (navy, sky blue, white accents)
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif
- **Navigation**: Sticky top navbar with smooth scroll to sections
- **Mobile-responsive**: Fully responsive layout for all screen sizes
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-in effects on scroll
- **SEO**: Proper meta tags, semantic HTML, descriptive headings
- **CTAs**: "Hire Me" / "Get in Touch" buttons placed strategically throughout

## Technical Notes
- No backend needed — fully static frontend
- Contact form will show a success toast (no email sending without a backend)
- All content hardcoded based on the VA Guide
